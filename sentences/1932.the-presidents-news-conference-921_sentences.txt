RESIGNATION OF JUSTICE OLIVER WENDELL HOLMES
THE PRESIDENT.

"In accordance with the provision of the Judicial Code as amended Section 260--Title 28, United States Code 375, I tender my resignation as Justice of the Supreme Court of the United States of America.

Sentence 4: But the time has come, and I bow to the inevitable.
