Sentence 1: 1903 Remarks Victorville California

My Fellow-Citizens:
I want to say what a pleasure it is to see you.
Sentence 2: I am enjoying so much coming into California.
Sentence 3: I have looked forward toward visiting your wonderful and beautiful State for years, and I am so glad of having the chance of being here.
Sentence 4: I welcome you all.
Sentence 5: I am glad to see the men, the women, and especially the children, for I believe in your stock and I am glad it is being kept up.
