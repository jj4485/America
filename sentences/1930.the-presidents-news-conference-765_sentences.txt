GOVERNMENT EXPENDITURES
THE PRESIDENT.

Sentence 8: SOUTH AMERICAN AIRMAIL SERVICE
Sentence 9: And I have a question on the South American air service.
Sentence 10: That service is cleared up.
Sentence 11: The contracts are now let on both coasts so that we now have a line down the east coast going through Rio as far as Buenos Aires, and a line from Santiago--or rather to put it the other way, from Miami, through Central America, and down the west coast as far as Santiago.
Sentence 12: The west coast is twice a week, and the east coast line once a week, so that we now have a complete service on both coasts operating regularly.
Sentence 13: That is all I have got in my mind.
