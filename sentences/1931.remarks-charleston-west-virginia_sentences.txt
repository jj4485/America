Sentence 3: It is again a gracious proof of the hospitality of the people of West Virginia.
Sentence 4: I have greatly enjoyed the journey over the State this morning.
Sentence 5: The scenery, the homes of West Virginia are always inspiring.

Sentence 7: This important Federal project is now nearing the stage of completion.
Sentence 8: I believe it will greatly aid the progress of the State.
Sentence 9: But I am not here to address you upon serious subjects.
Sentence 10: I have to deliver a serious speech tonight.
Sentence 11: I do wish to express to you the gratitude of Mrs. Hoover and myself for the kindliness and cordiality of your welcome.
