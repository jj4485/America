Sentence 1: 1931 Christmas Greeting The Nation 10

Mr. Vice President, the people of Washington and of our whole country-wide:
Sentence 2: This is the season and this is the occasion when the whole Nation unites in good cheer and good wishes.
Sentence 3: We dedicate it particularly to our children, and we give devotion to the faith from which it is inspired.
Sentence 4: It gives me great pleasure to join in this ceremony of the lighting of this tree, which is indeed symbolic of that in every household in our country.
Sentence 5: I ardently wish to every home a Merry Christmas and a Happy New Year.
