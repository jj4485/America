Sentence 1: 1931 Remarks Ponce Porto Rico

Fellow citizens:
I deeply appreciate your welcome and I am extremely glad of the opportunity to visit you and learn more about your problems.
Sentence 2: I am glad to visit the only part of the United States visited by Columbus.
Sentence 3: We regard it as the oldest part of the United States.
Sentence 4: It is also a great satisfaction to confer with your able Governor, whose distinguished father was the first to promote the welfare of this island.
Sentence 5: I have been greatly impressed by the courage with which you have faced your difficulties.
Sentence 6: I only wish I could stay longer and enlarge the acquaintance of my fellow citizens here.
